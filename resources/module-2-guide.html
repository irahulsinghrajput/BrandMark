🤖 Key AI Tools for Marketing

ChatGPT (OpenAI)
Best for: Content creation, copywriting, brainstorming
Cost: Free or $20/month Premium
Claude (Anthropic)
Best for: Research, analysis, detailed writing
Cost: Free or API pricing
Google Gemini
Best for: Multimodal tasks, integration with Google Workspace
Cost: Free tier available
DALL-E (OpenAI)
Best for: Marketing visuals, social media graphics
Cost: $15/month credits
Midjourney
Best for: High-quality artistic visuals
Cost: Starting at $10/month

    💡 Marketing Applications of Gen AI

    • Content Creation: Blog posts, social posts, email campaigns
    • Visual Content: Graphics, mockups, product images
    • Customer Service: Chatbots, automated responses
    • Research & Analysis: Competitor analysis, trend research
    • Personalization: Customized recommendations for users
    • Video Scripts: Outlines and scripts for video content

    ⚖️ AI Ethics Checklist

  • Disclose when content is AI-generated
  • Verify AI-generated facts and sources
  • Don't use AI to deceive or manipulate
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property
  • Use AI responsibly to enhance, not replace human creativity
  • Monitor for bias in AI outputs
