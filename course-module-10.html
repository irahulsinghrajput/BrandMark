1. Social Media Marketing Overview

Social media is where your audience spends their time. Platforms like Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Twitter are essential for brand building and customer engagement.

Platform Selection

  • Instagram: Visual content, younger demographics, e-commerce
  • LinkedIn: B2B, professional content, thought leadership
  • TikTok: Short video, viral content, Gen Z
  • Twitter: News, real-time engagement, industry discussions
  • YouTube: Long-form video, tutorials, entertainment

2. Content Strategy

Plan your content around your audience and business goals.

Content Pillars: Educational, Entertaining, Inspirational, Promotional (ratio: 40%, 30%, 20%, 10%)

Content Calendar

  • Plan 30-90 days in advance
  • Mix content types
  • Schedule posts for optimal times
  • Plan for trending topics

3. AI Content Creation Tools

  • Generate caption ideas with ChatGPT
  • Create images with DALL-E, Midjourney
  • Schedule posts with Buffer, Later
  • Analyze trending content with AI analytics

4. Engagement and Community Management

  • Respond to comments and DMs quickly
  • Use polls and questions to encourage interaction
  • Host Q&A sessions and live streams
  • Build genuine relationships with followers

5. Analytics and Optimization

  • Track Metrics: Reach, engagement, clicks, conversions
  • Identify Top Content: See what resonates
  • Test and Learn: A/B test different content types
  • Optimize Posting Times: Post when audience is most active

Key Takeaways

  • Choose platforms where your audience spends time
  • Create diverse content using the 40-30-20-10 rule
  • Use AI to generate and schedule content efficiently
  • Engage authentically with your community
  • Track metrics and optimize based on performance
