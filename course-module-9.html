1. Performance Marketing Fundamentals

Performance marketing is about paying only for desired results. You pay per click, impression, conversion, or other measurable action.

Key Advantages

  • Pay only for results
  • Easily track ROI
  • Scale what works
  • Flexible budgeting
  • Data-driven optimization

2. Attribution Models

Attribution determines which touchpoint receives credit for a conversion.

Common Models:
First-Touch (credits first interaction), Last-Touch (credits final interaction), Linear (equal credit to all), Time-Decay (more weight to recent interactions), Position-Based (40% first, 40% last, 20% middle)

3. Key Performance Metrics

  • Click-Through Rate (CTR): % of impressions that get clicked
  • Cost Per Click (CPC): Average cost per click
  • Cost Per Acquisition (CPA): Average cost to acquire one customer
  • Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): Revenue per dollar spent
  • Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): Total revenue per customer

4. Conversion Tracking Setup

Proper tracking is essential for optimization. Set up conversion pixels to measure:

  • Website purchases
  • Form submissions
  • App installs
  • Phone calls
  • Add to cart actions

5. Campaign Optimization Strategies

Testing

  • A/B test headlines, images, calls-to-action
  • Test landing pages
  • Test audience segments
  • Test bid strategies

Audience Refinement

  • Remove underperforming segments
  • Double down on high-converting audiences
  • Create lookalike audiences
  • Use retargeting for warm leads

6. Budget Allocation

Allocate budget based on performance. Invest more in channels and campaigns that deliver the best ROI.

7. AI and Machine Learning in Performance Marketing

  • Predictive analytics forecast likely converters
  • Automated bidding optimizes for conversions
  • Dynamic creative optimization tests variations
  • Smart audience targeting finds similar customers

Key Takeaways

  • Performance marketing focuses on measurable results
  • Proper attribution tracking is critical
  • Continuous testing drives improvement
  • AI helps optimize bids and targeting automatically
  • Monitor metrics and adjust budget allocation based on ROI
